Sakila Database Example

The Sakila database is a sample database designed for learning and testing purposes. It represents a film rental system and contains several tables related to films, customers, rentals, and more.

Tables

The Sakila database consists of several tables that manage different aspects of the film rental system. Here are some of the key tables:

  • actor - Information about actors.
  • category - Categories of films.
  • customer - Details of customers.
  • film - Information about films.
  • rental - Records of film rentals.
  • staff - Details of staff members.
  • store - Information about stores.

Relationships

The Sakila database tables are related to each other through various foreign keys. Here are some key relationships:

  • film_actor - Connects films and actors.
  • inventory - Connects films and stores.
  • rental - Connects customers and inventory.
  • payment - Records payments related to rentals.
